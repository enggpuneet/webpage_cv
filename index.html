About Me

My research interest is to understand how we deal with the motor redundancy, how do we learn new motor skills, and how do these mechanisms deteriorate with aging or neurological diseases. Unfortunately, we do not have answers to any of these questions. With this motivation and research background, I am combing approaches from robotics and neuroscience to identify the control mechanisms, brain plays. In future, I would use this control mechanism to make motor rehabilitation more mechanistic.
